 >Do’s
 -Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
 -Protect your privacy—use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings
 -Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
 -Be positive and supportive in your interactions with others
 -Manage your time—set limits to avoid excessive usage
 
 >Don’ts
 -Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
 -Don’t engage in online arguments, trolling, or cyberbullying
 -Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
 -Don’t believe or spread rumors without checking facts
 -Don’t let social media affect your studies or mental well-being
